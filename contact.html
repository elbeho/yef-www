YAPC::Europe Contact Information

How to contact us:

Website

http://www.yapceurope.org

Mailing Lists

conferences@yapceurope.org

The main list about YAPCs in Europe. The posting frequency usually increases inversely proportional to the time left until the next YAPC starts. Topics include calls for papers/participation, hotel room coordination and general discussions about the location/city.

To subscribe to the list send an empty email to conferences-subscribe@yapceurope.org or use the web interface: http://lists.yapceurope.org/mailman/listinfo/conferences. You can also use this interface to unsubscribe and to manage your subscription setting.

workshop-orgas@yapceurope.org

Closed list for people organising Perl workshops. If you plan to organise a workshop and want to join this list, please contact workshops@yapceurope.org.

Important Email Adresses

committee@yapceurope.org

For general questions regarding YAPCs (e.g. proposal to organise a YAPC) and the YAPC Europe Foundation.

workshops@yapceurope.org

For workshop releated questions: getting onto workshop-orgas, request support for organising a workshop ...
