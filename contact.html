YAPC::Europe Contact Information

How to contact us:

Website

http://www.yapceurope.org

Mailing List for announcements and discussions

conferences@yapceurope.org

The main list about YAPCs in Europe. The posting frequency usually increases inversely proportional to the time left until the next YAPC starts. Topics include calls for papers/participation, hotel room coordination and general discussions about the location/city.

To subscribe to the list send 'subscribe conferences' in the body of an email to majordomo@yapceurope.org.
To unsubscribe, send 'unsubscribe conferences' to the same address. Or send a mail containg 'help' for detailed instructions.

workshop-orgas@yapceurope.org

Closed list for people organising Perl workshops. If you plan to organise a workshop and want to join this list, please contact Thomas Klausner (domm@zsi.at).

Email the committee directly

committee@yapceurope.org
