The primary ways in which we can assist conference organizers are: (complimentary) payment processing, advice, and donations.

Conferences

YAPC conferences are designated by the Venue committee. Potential organizers should submit a proposal as described in the published call for proposals, which is typically released in the late winter or early spring. To make certain that you address the requirements and concerns of the committee, you are advised to review previous proposals:
  • Vienna (Y::E 2007)

Conference organizers are welcome to use the French Perl Monger's Act system in conjunction with YEF's backend. This system provides registration and also payment processing.

YAPC conferences can apply for a donation of up to 1000 Euros.
