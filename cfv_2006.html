Call for Venue "YAPC::Europe::2006 Conference"

While the Braga Perl Mongers are organizing the YAPC::Europe::2005 conference in Portugal, with exceptional enthusiasm and much feedback, the "YAPC::Europe Foundation" is already looking for the candidates for the following year.

If possible, the committee would like to announce the host city and country for 2006 at the end of the current (2005-Braga) conference. Any dedicated crew interested in organizing the YAPC::Europe conference in 2006 should send an informal application, stating their interest, to:

"YAPC::Europe::2006 conference proposal" <committee@yapceurope.org>

Questions about the application and help to present it can also be asked at this same address.

Deadline for applications: June 30th 2005

For general information about the venue requirements, check the YAPC::Venue module on CPAN, and look at the previous venues.

Norbert Gruener (President)
for YAPC Europe Foundation (YEF)
