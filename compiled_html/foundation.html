YAPC Europe Foundation - About the Foundation

Founded in the Netherlands, as the 'YAPC::Europe Foundation' or 'YEF', the current board also has members from England, Germany and France, plus subcommitee members from other countries.

The YAPC::Europe Foundation is not a branch of The Perl Foundation, but sometimes works in conjunction with TPF. YEF focuses on a narrower range of activities than TPF.

Our goal is to provide a focal point for Perl events in Europe. To this end, we offer an on-line registration system, mailing lists, advice, and may supply limited financial assistance. We look forward to being of service to like-minded Perl enthusiasts.
