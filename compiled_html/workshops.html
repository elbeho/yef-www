YAPC Europe Foundation - Workshops

Perl workshops differ from conferences in that they are typically smaller, and aimed at a local market. Therefore, they are often conducted in a local language, although they often include English material too, and may be targeted toward less experienced programmers. Workshops also tend to be smaller than conferences, but not as focused as hackathons.

To add your workshop to this list, or to contact the workshop committee for other reasons, please mail workshops@yapceurope.org.
