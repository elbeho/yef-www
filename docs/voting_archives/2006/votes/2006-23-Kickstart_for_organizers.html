Organizers of a YAPC or a Workshop can request a "kickstart". The default maximum kickstart for a YAPC is 1000 euro, for a Workshop is 500 euro. DISCUSSION

Philippe suggested to clearly state who is making the decision on giving the kickstart or not. The dicussion reminded everyone that, according to proposal 5, only the officials can decide on monetary issues.

REVISED PROPOSAL TEXT

Organizers of a YAPC or a Workshop can request a "kickstart". The default maximum kickstart for a YAPC is 1000 euro, for a Workshop is 500 euro. The request has to be accompanied with an explanation why the kickstart is needed. The kickstart is not a loan, but sponsorship and should be noted as such on the requesters website. The kickstart will be approved, modified or rejected by the YEF officials, which will be advised by the conference committee. The organizers are not required to donate any money to YEF after their event, but they are encouraged to do so, as a gentlemen's agreement and to encourage other YEF activities.
