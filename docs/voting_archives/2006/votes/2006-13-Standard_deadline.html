PROPOSAL TITLE

Standard deadline

DATE

Thu, 28 Sep 2006 14:46:39 +0200

PROPOSAL AUTHOR

Norbert Grüner

PROPOSAL TEXT

The standard deadline will be two weeks. The board can set up a list of issues with other deadlines, for instance to approve of press release texts. In case a board member announced a period of inavailability when such a deadline is set, that board member's vote will be valid when it is cast after the deadline.

ELECTIVES

The board.

DEADLINE

Deadline for votes: Monday October 2nd 2006 12:00 UTC

DISCUSSION

Elizabeth wondered if the inavailability clause was good to have, since it may change the outcome after the deadline.

Wendy noted that proposal 13 contradicted proposal 16.

REVISED PROPOSAL TEXT
