PROPOSAL TITLE

Report about financial status.

DATE

Mon, 25 Sep 2006 14:47:15 +0200

PROPOSAL AUTHOR

Norbert Grüner

PROPOSAL TEXT

The board needs to be informed regularly of the financial status of the foundation. Therefore a spreadsheet (or other database format) has to be made with all financial transactions made by the foundation (not including all separate ACT transmissions on behalf of the YAPC and workshop events).

In the Birmingham minutes an extensive list of information is mentioned that needs to be included. This spreadsheet must be made and supplied to the board members once every quarter. At least once a year these finances need to be discussed by the officials (normally, this means that only the big and/or unusual money transfers will be discussed, and "discussed" normally means "additional info by the treasurer on top of the 1 or 2 line description").

Until now, this has not be done, so a date has to be set on which we have to do this. Therefore we will request the treasurer to make such a spreadsheet and prepare the finance discussion. It should be done in two steps. Preparation of a short list within 4 weeks and of the extended list within 10 weeks.

The short list should contain for each transaction:

  • date =item * name of recipient/sender =item * Gross amount =item * One or two word classification (examples: Office Supply; Bank Costs...) =back

    Deadline for the short list: October 31st 2006

    Deadline for the extended list: December 11st 2006
