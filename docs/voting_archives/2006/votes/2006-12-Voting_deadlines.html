PROPOSAL TITLE

Voting deadlines

DATE

Thu, 28 Sep 2006 14:46:39 +0200

PROPOSAL AUTHOR

Norbert Grüner

PROPOSAL TEXT

When the board has to decide on any issue, the board sets a deadline for their members to reach a decision. Until that deadline, board members can discuss all aspects of the issue, put forward their objections, investigate alternatives, prices, pro's, con's, etc. When the deadline is reached, all board members vote. The whole board will accept the outcome of the vote.

ELECTIVES

The board.

DEADLINE

Deadline for votes: Monday October 2nd 2006 12:00 UTC

DISCUSSION

Ann wondered how the board as a whole could set a deadline.

Elizabeth noted that the text should say that "All board members should vote before the deadline is reached".

Norbert proposed the revised text below.

REVISED PROPOSAL TEXT

When the board has to decide on any issue, the board member who made the proposal sets a deadline for the board members to reach a decision. Until that deadline, board members can discuss all aspects of the issue, put forward their objections, investigate alternatives, prices, pro's, con's, etc. All board members should vote before the deadline is reached. The whole board will accept the outcome of the vote.
