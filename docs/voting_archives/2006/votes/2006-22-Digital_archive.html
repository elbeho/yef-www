PROPOSAL TEXT

Digital archives: due to the international nature of the foundation, all official documents have to become available online (in a password protected section in a repository). So these documents need to be scanned. This includes:

  • Official statutes and certified translations;

  • Official correspondence with clients and suppliers;

  • Tax papers;

  • Chamber of Commerce papers;

  • Financial papers: bank account statements, invoices to clients, invoices from suppliers, etc.
