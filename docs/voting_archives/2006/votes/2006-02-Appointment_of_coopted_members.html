PROPOSAL TITLE

Appointment of coopted members.

DATE

Monday September 18th 2006 13:35:28 +0200

PROPOSAL AUTHOR

Norbert Grüner

PROPOSAL TEXT

This proposal was stated during the Birmingham meeting on August 31st.

The officials can appoint co-opted board members, or "coops". Coops can vote on all but a select number of topics, their vote has the same value as a vote of an official.

ELECTIVES

The officials.
