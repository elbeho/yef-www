Venue

YAPC::Europe Venue Committee Page


The YAPC::Europe Venue Committee is here to discuss the city/country to host the next YAPC::Europe from the available proposals. Members are all, previous or current, conference organisers, so there is a great deal of relevant experience on the committee. Other tasks include advising the YEF board on kickstart sponsorship, and liasing with the chosen venue during the planning phase. YAPC::Europe::2007 will be held in Vienna, Austria. Initial proposals are welcomed for the following year (2008) at any time, and an official YEF - Call for Venue - 2008 will be posted at the appropriate time.
