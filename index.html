The YAPC::Europe Foundation is here to provide support for Perl conferences in Europe. The YAPC::Europe website (here :) is (still under construction...):

http://www.yapceurope.org

We're not an offshoot of YAS :
our purpose is more focused on conference related business.

Of course, though, we work hand in hand for the benefit of all.

The aim is to provide a focal point, organise on-line registration, mailing lists, technical and conference advice and to generally support activities, encouraging Perl Conferences and Workshops in Europe and environs:

Conferences
http://www.yapceurope.org/conferences

Workshops
http://www.yapceurope.org/workshops

Founded in the Netherlands, as the ``YAPC::Europe Foundation'' or 'YEF', the current board also has members from England, Germany and France. Adhoc committee members will be co-opted from relevant countries based on experience.

We look forward to helping, guiding and just plain 'being there' for groups of like-minded Perl enthusiasts in and around Europe.
