YAPC::Europe::2004 in Belfast

YAPC::Europe::2004 will be held in Belfast, Northern Ireland, from the 15th to 17th September. The theme will be Perl for Profit, using a liberal definition of "profit".


Previous European Conferences

  • YAPC::Europe::2003 was held in Paris

  • YAPC::Europe::2002 was held in Munich, theme "The Science of Perl"

  • YAPC::Europe::2001 was held in Amsterdam, theme "Security and Perl"

  • YAPC::Europe::2000 was held in London, theme "The Art of Perl"
