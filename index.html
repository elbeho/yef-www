The YAPC::Europe Foundation (YEF) is a non-profit organization which works to promote Perl in Europe primarily through public events.

YEF supports conference organizers with kickstart donations and by sponsoring an online payment system that can be connected to Act.

YEF is not affiliated with The Perl Foundation (TPF), which operates worldwide and has a broader mission of supporting Perl. However, the two organizations sometimes cooperate on European events. YEF encourages the use of conferencing software, like Act for example, to make management of resources easier - which solution to use is of course at the discretion of the organisers of each event.
