[% META title = 'Conferences' %] YAPC conferences are annual events for each regional area. Typically, each [% venue %] is at a new host city each year. By contrast, [% workshops %] are available in more locations, and are often held in the same location each year. While conferences appeal most to the intermediate to expert level, many offer a beginner's track or tutorials. Most conferences are conducted in English, while [% workshops %] tend to be local language based (usually plus English). Sometimes a conference may also include a [% hackathon %].
