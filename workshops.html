Perl workshops are typically smaller than [% conferences %] but not as focused as [% hackathons %]. They try to attract a local audience, and are consequently often targeted toward less experienced programmers.

Additionally, workshops are often conducted in a local language, although many may also include some material in English. In the table below, English is listed as a second language if an English speaker could attend a significant number of the talks; please refer to the workshop's pages to read the policy on submitting English-language proposals.

To add your workshop to this list, or to contact the workshop committee for other reasons, please mail workshops@yapceurope.org.
